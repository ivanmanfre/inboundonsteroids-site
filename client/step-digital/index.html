MTMark TranFounder, Step Digital · 1stScheduled · Mon 6 Jul · 🌐
The cheapest agency I ever lost a client to cost them around $40k. They switched to save $1,500 a month on management. Eight months later the account was doing half the revenue it used to. Owners fixate on the cost they can see. A management fee sits on an invoice every month, easy to point at, easy to cut. The growth it was driving never appears anywhere until it stops. I've watched brands spend weeks negotiating a $2,500 fee down to $2,000 while the campaigns under that fee drove a third of their revenue. One number sat on an invoice. The other just vanished from the top line.
